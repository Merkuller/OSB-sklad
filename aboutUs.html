11 years of work. Specialized warehouses for OSB

OSB-SLAD is a young company that is constantly growing and developing. For 11 years of work, we have already opened warehouses with oriented strand board in different cities. The largest warehouses reach 5.000 m2, where the best representatives of OSB in Russia and the CIS countries are located.

Over the years, work with us has been appreciated by thousands of different companies and tens of thousands of individuals. We work for the result and our main goal is to make the client satisfied and recommend us to his friends. That is why all boards arriving at our warehouses are thoroughly checked and only then shipped to customers. During the delivery of products to customers, we also guarantee maximum transparency and openness. Plates are delivered daily and several times to different parts of cities and to nearby cities / towns / villages.


We are the official representatives of manufacturing plants, thus we guarantee the best price and the best quality! Thank you for choosing our company! We appreciate this and will do our best to ensure that our relationship only goes in a positive direction!
